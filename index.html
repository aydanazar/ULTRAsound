Our Mission

Our Goals

What we hope to accomplish with ULTRAsound

Faster Diagnoses

By assisting the radiologists in generating reports, more patients can receive preventative care in a timely manner

Driving Engagement

Reducing paperwork is a direct action against a leading cause of burnout reported by radiologists

Universal Reporting

Utilizing industry language to ensure findings are reported to industry standards, efficiently

Informed Conversations

Our reporting feature can integrate a patient-friendly translation of medical terms, allowing patients to stay informed and confident about their care
